# How to Renew Your Demo License

![How to Renew Your Demo License image 01](./images/quick-guide-how-to-renew-your-demo-license_01.png)

## Introduction

This document explains how to renew your demo license for StarWatch SMS.

## SMS License Folder

The license for StarWatch SMS can be found in the following folder:
*C:\ProgramData\SMS*
as:
*StarWatch_SMS.lic*

![How to Renew Your Demo License image 02](./images/quick-guide-how-to-renew-your-demo-license_02.png)

## Move License

Move the license file from the folder listed above to the desktop or your documents folder.

## Restart the StarWatch SMS Management Console

When starting the *Management Console*, you will be prompted for a new license key.

## Re-enter Your Product Key

Simply re-enter the product key originally used and it will provide a new demo period. The key can also
be found inside the saved *.lic* file. Locate the line of text with the label “Purchase Code” and you can
cut and paste the license key.
