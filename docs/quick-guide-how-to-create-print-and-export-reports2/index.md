# How to Create, Print, and Export Reports

![How to Create, Print, and Export Reports image 01](./images/quick-guide-how-to-create-print-and-export-reports2_01.png)

Step 1 – The first step in the creation of a new report is to add a new search by clicking the “plus”
button at the top of the *Events* tab.

![How to Create, Print, and Export Reports image 02](./images/quick-guide-how-to-create-print-and-export-reports2_02.png)

### Step 2 – Next, click on *Filter*s to call up a list of search options.

![How to Create, Print, and Export Reports image 03](./images/quick-guide-how-to-create-print-and-export-reports2_03.png)

![How to Create, Print, and Export Reports image 04](./images/quick-guide-how-to-create-print-and-export-reports2_04.png)

Step 3 – Select the filters you would like to apply. In the example below, the *Audit* option has been
selected from the *Types* field.

![How to Create, Print, and Export Reports image 05](./images/quick-guide-how-to-create-print-and-export-reports2_05.png)

Step 4 – In the *Events* pane, select the types of events you would like to include in your report. In the
example below, all of the sub-categories under *Operator attendance* have been selected.

![How to Create, Print, and Export Reports image 06](./images/quick-guide-how-to-create-print-and-export-reports2_06.png)

![How to Create, Print, and Export Reports image 07](./images/quick-guide-how-to-create-print-and-export-reports2_07.png)

### Step 5 – Next, click the *View* button.

![How to Create, Print, and Export Reports image 08](./images/quick-guide-how-to-create-print-and-export-reports2_08.png)

### Step 6 – Rename your search and *Save* it for future use.

![How to Create, Print, and Export Reports image 09](./images/quick-guide-how-to-create-print-and-export-reports2_09.png)

![How to Create, Print, and Export Reports image 10](./images/quick-guide-how-to-create-print-and-export-reports2_10.png)

### Step 7 – Make your report by selecting it from your saved searches list.

![How to Create, Print, and Export Reports image 11](./images/quick-guide-how-to-create-print-and-export-reports2_11.png)

### Step 8 – Next, click *Report*.

![How to Create, Print, and Export Reports image 12](./images/quick-guide-how-to-create-print-and-export-reports2_12.png)

### Step 9 – Specify a time range and click *Run report*.

![How to Create, Print, and Export Reports image 13](./images/quick-guide-how-to-create-print-and-export-reports2_13.png)

![How to Create, Print, and Export Reports image 14](./images/quick-guide-how-to-create-print-and-export-reports2_14.png)

### Step 10 – Review the report and *Print* or *Export*.

![How to Create, Print, and Export Reports image 15](./images/quick-guide-how-to-create-print-and-export-reports2_15.png)

![How to Create, Print, and Export Reports image 16](./images/quick-guide-how-to-create-print-and-export-reports2_16.png)
